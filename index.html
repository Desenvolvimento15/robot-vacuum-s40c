*5,000Pa powerful suction fan blower: When fully charged and with "Turbo mode" enabled, the fan can reach a maximum vacuum degree of 5,000Pa using a vacuum suction meter. The measurement range is 0–35kPa with a precision of 0.01kPa. Performance may vary depending on actual usage conditions.
*520mL dustbin: Small plastic particles were put into the dustbin until they overflowed. The weight was then recorded. After three identical tests, the results showed that the dustbin capacity is ≥520mL. Performance may vary depending on actual usage conditions.
*260mL water tank: The water tank was filled with clean water and poured out for measurement with an electronic scale. The measurement results showed that the capacity of the clean water tank is 260mL. Performance may vary depending on actual usage conditions.
*20mm: Data tested by the manufacturer. When the Xiaomi Robot Vacuum S40C's mop pad is dry, the vacuum can traverse vertical steps up to 20mm in height. Performance may vary depending on actual usage conditions.
